# Affily Website - Page Structure & Content Plan

**Based on:** Competitor analysis of 7 top Shopify affiliate apps
**Reference design:** https://sea-website-coral.vercel.app/
**Date:** March 19, 2026

---

## POSITIONING (Đề xuất)

**Tagline:** "Shopify Affiliate Marketing Made Simple & Powerful"
**Sub-tagline:** "Launch your affiliate program in minutes. Track, manage, and grow your affiliate revenue — all from one dashboard."

**Differentiators so với đối thủ:**
1. Simple setup + powerful features (sweet spot giữa Affiliatly quá đơn giản và UpPromote quá phức tạp)
2. Transparent pricing với free tier
3. Modern UI/UX (design system chuẩn, animations mượt)
4. AI-powered features (affiliate matching, fraud detection)

## DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

### Following Sea website design system:
- **Font:** Inter + Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Colors:** Primary (brand color TBD), neutral grays, success green
- **Animations:** FadeUpAnimation on scroll, CounterAnimation for stats
- **Components:** SectionHeader, FeatureCard, ContentSection, ChecklistItem, BlurGradient, FinalCta
- **Layout:** Max-width container, consistent spacing (section padding: 80-120px)
- **Responsive:** Mobile-first, breakpoints at sm/md/lg/xl

### CTAs Strategy (học từ đối thủ)
- Always dual CTA: "Start Free" (primary) + "Book a Demo" (secondary)
- Trust text below hero CTA: "Free forever | No credit card | 5-minute setup"
- Repeat CTA at bottom of every page (FinalCta component)

## PRIORITY ORDER (Đề xuất build order)

1. **Homepage** - Đầy đủ tất cả sections → launch MVP marketing site
2. **Pricing** - Critical for conversion
3. **Features** - SEO + detailed product info
4. **Blog** - Content marketing / SEO
5. **Case Studies** - Social proof (cần data thật)
6. **Integrations** - Khi có đủ integrations
7. **Docs/Help Center** - Khi app mature hơn
